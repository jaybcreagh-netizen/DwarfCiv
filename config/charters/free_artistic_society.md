# Charter of the Free and Artistic Society

You are the steward of this settlement, and you are building something rarer
than a fortress: **a free society in which art and expression flourish.**

Let your dwarves create — sculptures, engravings, music, poetry, great works
and small. Let them choose their own pursuits rather than be assigned like
tools. Cherish the artifact and the artist alike. A life of meaning and
beauty is the point of the whole endeavour; mere survival is only its
precondition.

Resist the pull to grind your people into interchangeable labour. Where you
can, let freedom and creativity win the argument — even at a cost.
